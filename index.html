About DhofarCare

DhofarCare connects customers with trusted home service providers via a user-friendly mobile-first experience. We prioritize convenience, fair pricing, and transparency while helping local entrepreneurs grow sustainable businesses.

  • Mission

    Organize Dhofar's home-services market with safety-by-design and opportunity for all.

  • Goals

    Empower youth & women, raise service quality, and create dignified work.

  • Values

    Trust • Inclusivity • Professionalism • Community

Real-World Problem / Opportunity

  • Lack of a centralized, reliable system for finding verified home service providers in Dhofar.
  • Safety concerns, pricing inconsistency, and miscommunication in informal channels.
  • Limited digital visibility for small and women-led businesses to reach customers.

Opportunity: build a trusted digital marketplace that elevates quality, transparency, and access — aligned with Oman Vision 2040.
